For the documentation of the design project, you and all of your =
colleages are asked to fill out a short questionnaire after eThe meeting =
was about all the parts of the design coming together in order to create =
a specification for the prototype. The Project Manager introduced the =
meeting, and then we each gave a presentation based on what we had =
discovered during out preparation time. The Industrial Designer provided =
a list of options for possible materials for the remote, which we =
narrowed down over the course of the meeting. The Interface Designer =
gave some suggestions of the interface for the remote, suggesting a spin =
wheel similar to that used on the ipod. I then presented my research on =
the latest trends, then there was group discussion where we decided on =
the materials and features of the remote. The Project Manager then =
summarised the meeting.

Decisions made at the meeting:
- To use a simple battery (combination of simplicity, practicality and =
necessity) *
- The use rubber for the outer case (possibly an interchangable cover) =
and to have it curved. The remotes should come in a variety of colours - =
inspired by the fruit and vegetable theme. Use the company colors for =
the buttons etc. *
- The interface is to consist of some push buttons, LCDs with spinning =
wheel, and voice recognition *
- Advanced integrated circuits will need to be used for the electronics
- Sample sender/sample speakers need to be investigated further.

No real problems occured during the meeting, other that a lack of =
knowledge as to the sample senders and sample speakers. These are not =
particularly important to my role.

The Designers are creating a prototype, including the look-and-feel =
design (Industrial Designer) and the user interface design (Interface =
Designer), I will be working on the Product Evaluation.

Cat
